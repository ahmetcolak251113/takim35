# **Product Name**

  ### **`Spot`**  
  

  ## Product Description
  Spot is an AI-powered mobile app that turns a user's physical closet into an intelligent digital wardrobe. Tracks users' clothing usage through outfit selfies and lets users share their outfits with others through the social feed. Spot can recommend outfits and lets users virtually try them.
We want to help people be conscious of their clothing usage with just a simple daily routine. Inspire and be inspired while doing it.
We believe reducing decision fatigue when selecting an outfit and trying on clothes before putting them on can help people save time and effort. 

  <details>
    <summary><h2>Product Features</h2></summary>

  <h2>Clothing Identification:</h2>
    <p>Spot allows users to upload their clothing items and tags them automatically. Creates a wardrobe that users can check anytime.</p>

  <h2>Clothing Tracking :</h2>
    <p>Spot enables users to track their clothing usage by simply taking a mirror selfie. It identifies the clothes user is wearing and updates usage details.</p>

  <h2>Social Platform:</h2>
    <p>Spot provides users a platform where they can share the outfits they wore that day and enables them to intearct with eachother. </p>

  <h2>LLM:</h2>
    <p>Spot has an LLM integration. It helps users to ask for outfit advice and generate post caption ideas.</p>

  <h2>Outfit Recommendation:</h2>
    <p>Spot assembles outfit combinations from the user's actual wardrobe items. Factors in weather, frequency of use, color coordination, and personal style preference.</p>

  <h2>Virtual Try-On:</h2>
    <p>Spot lets users see themselves wearing an item or full outfit from their digital wardrobe.</p>

  </details>

  <details>
    <summary><h2>Target Audience</h2></summary>
    <p>Spot's target audience is primarily style-conscious mobile users, likely teens to 30s, who want to be more conscious of the clothing items they own. This can help them not over consume and support them for clothing donation. People who want help deciding what to wear and enjoy sharing outfit content are a great match.</p>
  </details>
